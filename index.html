Madagascar


Madagascar is an island in the Idian Ocean, it is the fifth biggest island in the world, in the East of Africa. The people living in Madagascar are called Malagasy and the language that we use is called Malagasy too. The capital of Madagascar is Antananarivo, it also has five other provinces in addition to the capital, 23 regions and 18 tribes who speaks different languages but not very different, there are many similarities in the languages of the each different tribe's language, and the "Malagasy ofisialy" is the standard language used everywhere. To say hello in Malagasy "Akory aby! or salama!"
